About Tony Heneine CPA

A CPA-led advisory practice built for owners and decision-makers who require financial clarity, discretion, and long-term guidance.

Tony Heneine is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with over two decades of experience in financial management, auditing, and advisory.

Throughout his career, he has held senior leadership roles including Finance Manager and Chief Accountant in prominent Lebanese companies within the hospitality and contracting sectors. His expertise spans financial reporting, strategic planning, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

With academic foundations in both Accounting and Law, Tony offers a distinct perspective that allows clients to navigate complex financial and legal environments with confidence.

He works closely with business owners and decision-makers who require not just accounting services, but strategic financial insight and long-term guidance.
